About Us

In India, large amounts of edible food go to waste, including surplus from weddings, hotels, catering services, and unsellable produce. Despite high-quality food being discarded, millions still face hunger. The issue lies in distribution and coordination, not food quality. Our platform connects donors with NGOs and social workers to help redistribute surplus food to those in need, preventing waste and aiding the impoverished.
