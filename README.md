Project Description :

We are providing a platform that acts as a medium between food donors and people who suffer in poverty. It is one kind of foodbank in which food is recycled by donating surplus food for the good. People who wish to donate can fill in the details and social workers or NGOs that transport the food to the poor can claim it.


Problem Statement :

The wastage of edible food which are of high quality which is mostly a distribution and coordnation problem.


Solution :

Creating a website in which catering or any other related companies can donate extra food which is leftover from any of their events and the organisations working for the benefit of the poor, needy or homeless people can claim and transport it to them. This way quality food will not get wasted, people suffering in extreme poverty can get proper food and hence nourishments and also the social organisations which may or may not be acquiring a good income can do social services without fund concerns.
